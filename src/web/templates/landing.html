Usage Guide

The First Hour Range Analysis tool provides insights into market behavior during the crucial first hour of trading:

  • Recent Days Table: Shows the exact range (high minus low) for the first trading hour of the last 5 trading days
  • VIX Correlation: Analyzes how the VIX level affects first hour ranges
  • Day of Week Patterns: Reveals which days tend to have larger or smaller opening ranges
  • Distribution Analysis: Shows the frequency distribution of first hour ranges

The Gap Analysis tool helps understand market behavior around price gaps:

  • Gap Statistics: Probability of gaps filling based on historical data
  • Size Analysis: How gap size affects fill probability
  • Time Analysis: Average time taken for gaps to fill
  • Market Context: Relationship between gaps and market conditions

The Market Events Analysis tool examines significant market moves and their context:

  • Event Classification: Categorization of different types of market events
  • Impact Analysis: How events affect market behavior
  • Pattern Recognition: Common patterns around significant events
  • Historical Context: Similar historical events and their outcomes
